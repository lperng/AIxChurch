Wise Applications of AI in the Church

Biblically-Grounded Use Cases in Ministry, Evangelism, and Discipleship

Ministry Operations

Boost Efficiency, Focus on People

Wise Applications

  • Administrative Automation: Use an AI assistant to schedule meetings, send event reminders, and summarize meeting notes.
  • Resource Intelligence: Analyze donation and expense data to provide financial reports and budget suggestions.
  • Event Planning Support: Assist in planning staffing and material needs for large events based on past data.

Guiding Principles

Good Stewardship: Free up staff from tedious tasks to focus on the core ministry of care, prayer, and relationship-building.
Love Your Neighbor: Automation cannot replace personal care. For a member in need, a warm greeting is far better than a form message.

Digital Evangelism

Spread the Gospel, Reach All People

Wise Applications

  • Content Generation: Help brainstorm social media posts and short video scripts to express truth in contemporary language.
  • Cross-Cultural Missions: Quickly translate testimonies and gospel tracts to reach different language and cultural groups.
  • Seeker Q&A: Train an AI to provide initial answers to common faith questions, guiding them into deeper conversations with believers.

Guiding Principles

Wise Discernment: All AI-generated content must be manually reviewed for theological accuracy and faithfulness to Scripture. The goal is to lead people to Christ, not to chase clicks or online fame.
Glorify God: Ensure the message conveyed is pure truth, not a diluted, world-pleasing gospel.

Discipleship Support

Deepen Truth, Grow in Life

Wise Applications

  • Personalized Devotions: Generate custom reading plans and reflection questions based on an individual's spiritual stage and inquiries.
  • Study Material Prep: Quickly summarize different commentaries, related verses, and background for a specific passage as prep material for small group leaders.
  • Sermon Enrichment: Assist pastors in brainstorming sermon illustrations and organizing message outlines to enrich the pulpit message.

Guiding Principles

Beware of Idolatry: AI is a "tool" and cannot replace the Holy Spirit's guidance, a pastor's teaching, or the companionship of spiritual peers. The ultimate authority is always God's Word.
Love Your Neighbor: The core of discipleship is the real, life-on-life relationship, not a one-way transfer of knowledge.
